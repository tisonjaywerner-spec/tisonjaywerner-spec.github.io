About Me

I'm a Business Management, Administration and Operations student at Gustavus Adolphus College (graduating December 2026), focused on the intersection of data analysis, business analysis, process improvement, and database management. My work spans regulatory and managerial accounting, quality systems, inventory operations, and full-stack application development. My work follows the consistent thread of using data to find the root cause of a problem, communicating those findings, and then actually fixing it.

Recent highlights include analyzing internal control assessment lifecycles to identify potential process improvements at UnitedHealth Group, identifying $150,000+ in unresolved WIP balance errors at Delta Fabricating, and designing a normalized Azure SQL database from scratch for a full-stack fitness tracking application. All of these projects include measurable, documented outcomes.
